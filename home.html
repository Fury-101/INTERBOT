What is InterHackBot?

InterHackBot is a Discord bot specifically designed for online hackathons.
Use our many features to effciently work and enjoy your time in the hackathon!

Features

Language Convertor

Are your team-mates extremely bright in coding, but don't speak
the same language? The language feature helps you convert
____ including _____. You can get ahead of the competition
with the help of your smart teammate

Hackathon Timer

Get confused with the time schedule for online hackathons? Set up
to receive a count-down time reminder for your hackathon to
get hold of the time. This feature also includes a shortcut to send your
teammate a reminder to start working.

Personalized ID Card

For those who are still in need of a team and those who would like to
proudly display their stats this feature is for you! You can add various
stats including your experience level all the way to your hobbies.
Others who can see that you are still looking for teammates can also
react out to you, and you can get started right away!

Hackathon Infomation

Admins will be prompted to enter crucial hackathon information
in which participants can easily access and understand upon triggering
the command.

Team Management

Don't know who to start a team with? The team feature gives you the
ID's of everyone who is still looking for a team. Furthermore, it also has
fun team-builder activities that you can do on the first day to get to
know other people.
